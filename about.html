About Arunodaya High School

Established in 1987 and run under Sree Arkavathi Vidya Samasthi, Arunodaya High School is committed to delivering quality education to children of Kamakshipura Byatha and nearby areas. We emphasize moral values, academic rigour, and community participation.

Vision

To empower students with knowledge and character to face the future confidently.

Mission

  • Provide accessible, quality education.
  • Encourage extracurricular and sports activities.
  • Promote cultural and ethical values.
